QUEEN

Who wants to live forever?

Queen are a British rock band that formed in London in 1970. Their classic line-up was Freddie Mercury, Brian May, Roger Taylor, and John Deacon. Their earliest works were influenced by progressive rock, hard rock and heavy metal, but the band gradually ventured into more conventional and radio-friendly works by incorporating further styles, such as arena rock and pop rock, into their music. May and Taylor have performed under the Queen name with Paul Rodgers and Adam Lambert as vocalists on tours since.
